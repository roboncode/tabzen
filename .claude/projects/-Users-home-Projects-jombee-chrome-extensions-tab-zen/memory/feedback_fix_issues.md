name: Fix issues when discovered
description: Always fix pre-existing errors encountered during work, don't defer them
type: feedback
---

Always fix issues as they are discovered, including pre-existing errors. Don't defer or skip them just because they weren't caused by the current task.

**Why:** User expects proactive fixes — leaving known issues unfixed wastes time and creates technical debt.

**How to apply:** When TS errors, lint issues, or other problems surface during verification steps, fix them immediately rather than noting them as "pre-existing" and moving on.
